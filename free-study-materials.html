Frequently Asked Questions

Are all study materials really free? +

Yes, absolutely. Every single PDF, DPP, notes file, and book available here is 100% free to download. We believe quality education should never be locked behind a paywall, and we intend to keep it that way.

Where do I find the materials? +

All materials are shared through our official Telegram channels — @Jageryyyy and @Jageryyyy2. Join the channels to access and download files directly. The Telegram app is free on Android, iOS, and desktop.

Which exams are these materials useful for? +

Our materials are curated primarily for JEE Main, JEE Advanced, NEET UG, and Class 11–12 Board exams. However, the notes and DPPs are also beneficial for KVPY, Olympiads, and state-level competitive exams.

How often are new materials uploaded? +

We upload new resources multiple times each week. Our Telegram channels are the best way to stay updated in real time. Turn on notifications so you never miss a new upload.

Can I request specific materials? +

Yes! Drop a message or a request in our Telegram community. We take requests seriously and prioritise materials that multiple students are looking for. Community feedback drives our upload schedule.

Are there any ads on this site? +

No. This website is completely ad-free. We keep things clean and distraction-free so you can focus entirely on finding and downloading what you need.
